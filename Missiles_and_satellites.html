About The Missiles and satellites

Satellites are artificial objects placed in orbit around celestial bodies, commonly Earth. They serve various purposes, such as communication, Earth observation, navigation, and scientific research. Satellites can be categorized into different types, including communication satellites for transmitting signals, weather satellites for monitoring atmospheric conditions, and navigation satellites like GPS. They play a crucial role in modern technology, enabling global connectivity and facilitating a wide range of applications in telecommunications, weather forecasting, and more.

A rocket is a vehicle that propels itself through space by expelling exhaust gases in the opposite direction of its motion. It typically consists of a propulsion system, fuel tanks, and payload. Rockets are crucial for space exploration, satellite deployment, and various scientific endeavors.
